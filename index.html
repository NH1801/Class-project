SEVEN WONDERS OF THE WORLD

Historical places of India are the platforms to narrate the rich diversity of all sorts that have been the peculiarity of the nation. The enriching cultural and traditional diaspora since ages and their evolution can be understood. Each historical place or monument has a story to narrate and retell that captivates its tourists or enthusiasts wanting more.

TAJ MAHAL

A white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

GREAT WALL OF CHINA

A massive fortification stretching across China, built over centuries.

MACHU PICCHU

Ancient Incan citadel in Peru, known for its breathtaking scenery.

COLOSSEUM

Massive amphitheater in Rome, once hosting gladiatorial battles.

CHRIST THE REDEEMER

Iconic statue of Jesus in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

CHICHEN ITZA

Ancient Mayan pyramid in Mexico, known for its astronomical precision.

PETRA

Ancient rock-cut city in Jordan, famous for its intricate carvings.
